Our showroom features geospatial solutions from the public and tertiary students who have been recognized in the Category 4 awards.

This year's theme is "Environmental, Social, and Governance" (ESG), which addresses crucial issues for our city's sustainable future.

Contestants applied ESG principles to suggest improvements for our city in areas of sustainability, resilience, and diversity. The ESG themes touch upon environmental care, social inclusivity, and resource access, demonstrating the potential of spatial data in problem-solving.
